Employees are any business’ greatest and most important asset!

With Edupoles, employee training and development is no longer a hassle, due to its web-based LMS that supports E-Learning, Classroom Training, and Hybrid Learning with a unique formula to meet the users’ learning needs with its convenient, easy-to-use features.

Edupoles is beneficial for mid-sized and large organizations that are looking to systematically manage the learning and development of their staff and allowing them to allocate more resources in planning than administrative work.

Why Edupoles Business?

  • Cost Effective: Through e-learning, you can reduce many costs that are usually associated with traditional training; such as: training venue, travel and transportation, catering, trainers, …etc.
  • Time-Effective: Most traditional trainings are very time consuming; however, e-learning easily allows you to overcome this through its flexibility to be accessed anytime and anywhere, thus, ensuring that it doesn’t take away any of the employees’ valuable time to focus more on their work.
  • One Destination for All:
    • Edupoles gives employees one place to manage their performance and learning curve, and help them take responsibility for their career development. Additionally, they can easily update development plan status, request new trainings, and launch learning activities; as well as view current training courses available and enrol online.
    • As for managers, Edupoles enables them to engage employees in their learning and career development, through collaborating with them to create the right development plans and establish a path to improved performance and career progression over time.
  • High Retention of Learning and Knowledge: Blended learning leads to a higher retention rate of knowledge as opposed to pure classroom training. Another advantage is the ability to refresh and update any training materials whenever needed; thus, ensuring that the employees have constant access to it.
  • Customization: With Edupoles, you can customize your own online internal training courses using different formats; such as: videos, documents, presentations, exercises, and evaluation.

This enables you to focus more on the areas of development you want to tackle for each employee, as well as giving you the ability to integrate your brand within your training courses; thus, making your staff feel more invested in your business.

  • Facilitated Tracking: Edupoles allows you to track your employees’ development, managing their training and development plans, and having access to the learning and training history of each employee. Moreover, it facilitates reports of the employees’ training and development to higher management.
  • Flexibility: Which is a convenience to everyone; where employees can take their training courses with them anytime, anywhere, and managers have the luxury of tracking and keeping up with them whenever and wherever they want.
