About

Edupoles is an innovative, multifunctional cloud based LMS and E-learning solution that is designed to provide you with integrated and ease to use, scalable learning ecosystem to deliver ultimate learning experience with access to a growing course marketplace of high quality content and professional knowledge that matches your training requirements, industry and culture.

Edupoles is embraced for its unique approach of blending traditional learning method with the modern tools to increase efficiency, learning engagement and respond rapidly to dynamic changing market making premium technology and knowledge accessible and affordable to any single organization and individual worldwide.

Core beliefs

In the ideation process, we had a simple vision in mind : "Everyone who wants to learn, has the right to learn in a convenient way. No matter what are the blockers, our role is to unlock potentials and give learning access for everyone, in any field"

We connect those who want to learn to the sources of real knowledge they are looking for in the most convenient and flexible way. During the job, we learn, we evolve and we continue to make a bigger positive impact.We value people, Learning, passion and commitment.
